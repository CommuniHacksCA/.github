# CommuniHacks

CommuniHacks is a registered nonprofit in Ontario, Canada that hosts 
annual community hackathons where youth aged 15 to 24 build functional 
tech solutions for local nonprofits and community organizations in 
Waterloo Region.

## What We Do

We bring together university and high school students for a weekend of 
hands-on tech work. Each team is paired with a local nonprofit or 
community organization to build working software that solves a real 
challenge they face. After the event, participants and partners can 
continue collaborating to fully integrate and deploy their solution.

## Why It Matters

Many nonprofits in Waterloo Region struggle with outdated technology, but 
cannot afford technical talent. Talented youth across Ontario have technical skills, but do not have many opportunities to apply them in meaningful ways for our community. CommuniHacks bridges this gap by 
connecting skilled youth with community organizations that need their help most.
